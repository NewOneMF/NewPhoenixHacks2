FAQs

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where programmers, designers, and other tech enthusiasts come together to create a project in a short time frame.

Who can participate?

Anyone with an interest in technology and innovation is welcome to participate.

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop, chargers, and any other equipment you might need for coding and creating your project.

Are there any prizes?

Yes, there will be prizes for the best projects in various categories.
