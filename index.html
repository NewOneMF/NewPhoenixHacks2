Phoenix Hacks 2024

About the Event

Our annual hackathon is a 12-hour event where developers, designers, and entrepreneurs come together to create innovative projects from scratch. Whether you're a seasoned hacker or a beginner, there's a place for you at our event.

  • 01:00 PM - Lunch
  • 06:00 PM - Dinner

  • 11:00 PM - Award Ceremony and Closing
